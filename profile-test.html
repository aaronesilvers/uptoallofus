THE VENUE

A lovely resort on Lake Buchanan in Texas. Complete with adorable cabins and canoes (though that may be a bit cold). We will have the entire place to ourselves for bonfires, dance parties, cooking, ideating, co-creating, and other exponentially awesome activities. For more information on the location, sign in to the ticketing area with your password.


The resort is about an hour north of Austin, in Buchanan Dam, TX. Burnet and Kingsland are nearby towns with lots of restaurants and shops as well as wineries. Since the weekend includes everything you need including meals, you won't really need any of that, but options are nice. Otherwise, it's near hills and nature. Where, let's be honest, you want to be anyway.

UP TO ALL OF US

Up to All of Us is a community built around one thing: discovering obstacles and working out the paths to making things better. We gather people at the intersections of learning, technology, and design. People who are playing in the overlaps and influenced by the ragu of odd things interesting things that creep in along the way. This is not a one-off event, like a club you buy admission into; this is a community where everyone participates and the ripple effects continue throughout the year.


As a gathering, it is a thick spined and loosely structured experience where passion, engagement, and drive are required, learning and working together are the features; and action trumps monologue in every instance.

This is our second year. Read about what happened last year here. If anyone would like to secretly get married at Up to All of Us again, feel free to do so (we're not kidding).
